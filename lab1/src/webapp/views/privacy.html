Log Files
Like many other websites, articles.com makes use of log files. These files log visitors to the site – a standard procedure for hosting companies. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users' movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any personally identifiable information.
